ICAR Package of Practices — Pulses: Gram (Chickpea), Arhar (Pigeonpea), Moong, Urad
Source: ICAR-Indian Institute of Pulses Research (IIPR), Kanpur

══════════════════════════════════════
CHICKPEA / GRAM (Cicer arietinum)
Season: Rabi (October–March). Largest pulse crop of India.
══════════════════════════════════════

TYPES
Desi (small, dark seed): Most common. JG-11, JG-14, Pusa-256, Vijay.
Kabuli (large, cream seed): Higher price. JAKI-9218, Phule G-5.

SOWING
Optimal: October 20 – November 10. Avoid early sowing (Helicoverpa attack increases).
Spacing: 30×10 cm.
Seed rate: 60–80 kg/ha (Desi); 80–100 kg/ha (Kabuli).
Seed treatment: Rhizobium + PSB inoculant @ 5g/kg seed. Saves N fertilizer.
Fungicide seed treatment: Thiram 75WP @ 3g/kg (controls collar rot, dry root rot).

SOIL AND WATER
pH 6.0–8.5. Sandy loam to clay loam.
Gram is drought tolerant — mostly rainfed.
One irrigation at pre-flowering (45–50 DAS) increases yield by 25%.
NEVER irrigate at pod formation in humid weather — causes disease.

FERTILIZER
Low N requirement (N-fixing legume).
N:P:K = 20:60:20 kg/ha as basal.
Rhizobium inoculation reduces N need further.
Sulphur: 20 kg/ha on sandy soils.

MAJOR PESTS

Pod Borer (Helicoverpa armigera):
Most serious pest — larvae eat pods and seeds.
ETL: 1–2 larvae per meter row.
Monitoring: Pheromone traps 10/ha.
Control: Emamectin benzoate 5SG @ 0.4g/L OR Indoxacarb 14.5SC @ 0.5 mL/L.
Spray in evening (larvae feed at night).
Biological: Bt spray @ 2 kg/ha for young larvae.
Nuclear Polyhedrosis Virus (HaNPV) @ 100 LE/ha is very effective.

DISEASES

Fusarium Wilt:
Yellow wilting of plants. Death in patches.
Use resistant varieties: JG-74, ICCC-32.
Trichoderma soil application at sowing.

Collar Rot (Sclerotium rolfsii):
Water-soaked lesion at base, plant topples.
Avoid waterlogging. Seed treatment with Trichoderma.

YIELD: Rainfed 10–15 q/ha; Irrigated 20–25 q/ha.
MSP 2024-25: Rs. 5,650 per quintal.

══════════════════════════════════════
ARHAR / PIGEONPEA (Cajanus cajan)
Season: Kharif (June–January). Long duration 180–270 days.
══════════════════════════════════════

VARIETIES
Short duration (120–150 days): ICPL-87119 (Asha), GT-1.
Medium duration (150–180 days): Pusa 992, UPAS-120.
Long duration (270 days): IPA-203, T-21 (traditional).

SOWING
Optimal: June 15 – July 15.
Spacing: 90×20–30 cm (sole crop). Important: Wide spacing reduces disease.
Seed rate: 15–20 kg/ha.

FERTILIZER
N:P:K = 20:50:20 kg/ha as basal.
Rhizobium inoculation highly effective.

MAJOR PESTS

Pod Fly (Melanagromyza obtusa) and Pod Borer (Helicoverpa):
Control: Chlorpyrifos 20EC @ 2.5 L/ha at pod initiation.
Monitoring with pheromone traps.

YIELD: 12–20 q/ha.
MSP 2024-25: Rs. 8,000 per quintal.

══════════════════════════════════════
MOONG / GREEN GRAM (Vigna radiata)
Season: Kharif + Zaid (short duration 60–65 days — fits between crops).
══════════════════════════════════════

VARIETIES
Pusa Vishal, Samrat, IPM-02-3, Pusa-105 (Zaid), Pant Moong-5.

SOWING
Kharif: June–July. Zaid: February–March.
Spacing: 30×10 cm. Seed rate: 20–25 kg/ha.

FERTILIZER
N:P:K = 20:40:20 kg/ha.

MAJOR PEST: Yellow Mosaic Virus (YMV) — vector: whitefly.
Use resistant variety Pusa Vishal. Control whitefly.

YIELD: 8–12 q/ha.
MSP 2024-25: Rs. 8,682 per quintal.

══════════════════════════════════════
URAD / BLACK GRAM (Vigna mungo)
Season: Kharif. Sometimes summer crop.
══════════════════════════════════════

VARIETIES: T-9, Co-6, LBG-648.
Seed rate: 20–25 kg/ha. Spacing: 30×10 cm.
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 20:40:20 kg/ha.
Major disease: Yellow Mosaic Virus (YMV) — same management as moong.
YIELD: 8–12 q/ha.
MSP 2024-25: Rs. 7,400 per quintal.
